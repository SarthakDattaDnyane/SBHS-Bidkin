About Our School

Shri Saraswati Bhavan Prashala, Bidkin, is a premier education institution committed to providing quality education with a prefect blend of academics, sports, and cultural activities. Guided by the values of knowledge, discipline, and holistic development, our school strives to nurture young minds into responsible and successful individuals. With a well-structured curriculum and experienced faculty, we ensure that students receive a string foundation in academics while also encouraging creativity and critical thinking.

Our school is equipped with modern facilities, including well-equipped classrooms, science and computer labs, and a rich library, creating an environment conducive to learing. We strongly emphasize co-curricular activities, offering students opportunities to participate in sports, arts, debates, and cultural programs to develop their all-round skills. Along with education, we instill moral and ethical values, fostering discipline, respect, and leadership qualities in our students.

Our motto, "तमसो मा ज्योतिर्गमय" - From Darkness to Light, reflects our dedication to enlightening young minds through knowledge and wisdom. We offer a safe and supportive atmosphere where students can grow with confidence and explore their full potential. Located in Bidkin, Maharashtra, we welcome students to embark on a journey of learning and success with us. For admissions and inquiries, visit our school or contact us today!
